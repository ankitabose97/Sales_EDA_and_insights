# Sales_EDA_and_insights
This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis using Pandas, Seaborn and Matplotlib library to clean and analyze a sales dataset. The objective is to uncover meaningful business insights and present concise findings that can support stakeholders in making data-driven decisions to improve revenue growth and enhance customer experience.

## Goals of the Project:
  - Perform data cleaning and exploratory data analysis (EDA) using Pandas to identify patterns, trends, missing values, and inconsistencies in the sales dataset.
  - Use Matplotlib and Seaborn to create insightful visualizations that highlight sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends.
  - Generate actionable business insights and concise summaries to help stakeholders make data-driven decisions for improving revenue and customer experience.

## Outline of the Project:
  - i) Libraries import ii) Dataset exploration and Cleaning iii) Groupby tables iv) Visualization in Pandas  - Use Matplotlib and Seaborn to create insightful  visualizations 

## Questions:

In this project, I tried to give answers to a set of questions that may be relevant when analyzing Sales data:

 - What is the expenditure pattern of different genders?
 - What is the expenditure pattern of different age groups?
 - Does Marital status affect purchasing behavior?
 - What are the top 10 states in terms of order and revenue?
 - Occupation details of our buyers
 - What are the top-performing categories, and which products are driving most of the revenue?
 - In addition, I made a visual analysis to plan marketing campaigns more effectively.

## Insights:
*Unmarried women of the age group 26-35 years and mostly from UP, Maharashtra, and Karnataka, and working in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation are more likely to buy products from the Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories*
